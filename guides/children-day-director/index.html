The Step of Growth

Children Day Director

Children Day is one of the most central roles in Resala. It is where Resala works directly on children's educational, emotional, and developmental growth week by week.

Guiding question Can you design a full journey that helps children grow week by week, not just plan activities for one Saturday?

Role details

What this director actually carries

What you will actually do

  • Set the semester goals for Children Day.
  • Review previous mistakes and improve the plan.
  • Build the curriculum for the semester.
  • Plan weekly Saturday sessions.
  • Prepare or review slides, activities, worksheets, games, and materials.
  • Align heads and volunteers with the vision.
  • Make sure activities serve a clear goal.
  • Attend or strongly follow up on Saturdays.
  • Evaluate each week and improve the next one.
  • Track progress across the semester.

Leadership requirement

Lead heads and volunteers around a semester journey, not isolated Saturdays. Everyone should know the goal behind each activity.

Ownership requirement

Own the children's growth across time. Review mistakes, evaluate sessions, and improve the next week instead of repeating activities blindly.

Skills requirement

  • Curriculum planning.
  • Creativity.
  • Understanding children's needs.
  • Activity design.
  • Weekly evaluation.
  • Team alignment.
  • Patience and emotional awareness.
  • Ability to connect fun with purpose.
  • Strong follow-up and consistency.

Preferred experiences or passions

  • You care about children's growth and learning.
  • You can connect fun activities with a developmental purpose.
  • You are ready for weekly consistency and evaluation.

Interview task direction

Build a four-week Children Day journey: goal, weekly themes, activities, materials, volunteer roles, and how you would evaluate progress.
