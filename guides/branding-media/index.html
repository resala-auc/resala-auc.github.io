The Step of Voice

Branding / Media

Branding is how people see, understand, and remember Resala. If Resala does strong work but no one sees it clearly, the impact becomes less visible.

Guiding question Can you turn Resala's work into stories, visuals, and campaigns that people can see, trust, remember, and feel connected to?

Role details

What this director actually carries

What you will actually do

  • Build content campaigns and posting strategies.
  • Lead design, media, video editing, photography, captions, and reels.
  • Organize the branding team and assign clear tasks.
  • Cover events and turn moments into meaningful stories.
  • Make sure Resala's visual identity and tone are consistent.
  • Use content to support recruitment, trust, visibility, and impact.
  • Follow up from idea to final post.

Leadership requirement

Lead creative people with clear briefs, feedback, deadlines, and quality standards while protecting the dignity of service content.

Ownership requirement

Own how Resala is seen. Do not wait for content to appear; plan campaigns, capture moments, and turn work into clear stories.

Skills requirement

  • Creative direction.
  • Content planning.
  • Marketing awareness.
  • Storytelling.
  • Understanding of Instagram and reels.
  • Basic design or video editing understanding.
  • AI tools for content creation.
  • Ability to give feedback.
  • Ability to lead creative people.
  • Sensitivity and dignity in community service content.

Preferred experiences or passions

  • You care about storytelling, branding, media, and public image.
  • You understand what makes people trust and remember a page.
  • You enjoy turning real work into campaigns.

Interview task direction

Design a content campaign for Resala: goal, audience, message, post types, reels, captions, visual direction, and how it supports trust or recruitment.
